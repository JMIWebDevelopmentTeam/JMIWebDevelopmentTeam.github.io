B.Tech Programme

Course Overview:

Intake: 75
Programme/Course Fee : 10,850/year
Duration: 8 semesters (4 years)
Type: Full time

Admission process:
Admission to B.Tech programme is done on the basis of marks obtained by the candidate in the JEE Main examination, conducted by CBSE. Interested candidates, in addition to registering for JEE Mains, shall have to register at www.jmicoe.in. The application forms are made available on the university website in the month of Dec-Jan each year.
The list of selected/waitlisted candidates is usually declared in the first week of July.
