MISSION and VISION

Mission

Commitment to world class teaching, mentoring, intellectual stimulation, industry collaborations, outreach programmes and state-of-the-art research

Vision

To produce comprehensively trained, socially responsible, innovative computer engineers and researchers of highest quality to contribute to the nation’s imprint on the world stage.
