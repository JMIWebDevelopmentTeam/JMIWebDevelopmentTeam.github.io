M.Tech Programme

Course Overview:

Intake: 20
Programme/Course Fee: 13,050/year
Duration: 4 semesters (2 years))
Type: Full time

Admission process:
Jamia Millia Islamia conducts a separate entrance test for M.Tech admissions. Admissions are done solely on the basis of this entrance test.
Forms are tentatively made available in April.
Interested candidates are advised to check www.jmi.ac.in for M.Tech admission updates.

Stipend:
All GATE Qualified students enrolled in the M.Tech programme are eligible for a monthly scholarship of 12,400/year, provided by the Government of India.

Career Prospects:
The programme bears testimony to an excellent placement track record, with our students bagging offers from the top companies in the industry. The current placement season has saw the Exchanger company hiring at an annual package of close to a million.
The department also boasts of state-of-the-art laboratory and infrastructure facilities for its students, thereby stimulating an atmosphere of research among them.
